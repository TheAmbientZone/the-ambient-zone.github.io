Babar’s Bigger and Better Ambient Oracle

whose territory are you in?

describe a track across eight dials and the oracle will name which of ~40 artists' patch of the drone you've wandered onto. tape loops + profound loss = basinski. you know the drill.

load a track (optional)

drop in a track and the oracle will listen on your own device — brightness, texture, stereo width, harmonic centre, movement, pulse and length — then set the dials it can actually hear. you supply the rest (the sound source, the mood). nothing is uploaded; it even works with the wifi off.

analyse a whole set

drop in several tracks at once (or a whole set) and the oracle reads each one on-device, then shows how your spectral fingerprint drifts across the running order — handy for spotting where a set gets brighter, busier or loses its low end. nothing uploaded.

what is this, and is it safe to open?

What it is: I made this with AI — it's a little programme you open up in a browser. It's not a link to anything!! It just names which ambient artist a track sounds like, for a bit of fun.

Why it's safe: there's no link to tap, no sign-in, no payment, and nothing to download or install. It can't message anyone or take any details — everything runs right here on your own phone, even with the wifi off. Nothing leaves your device.

Opening it from WhatsApp: if it looks half-empty or frozen, you're viewing it inside WhatsApp's built-in browser, which won't run it properly. Tap the ⋮ menu (top-right) and choose Open in Chrome, or the share icon and Open in Safari — the dials and the verdict will then appear and work.

the constellation

every artist the oracle knows, mapped by how similarly the dials score them — computed live from the scoring table itself, no hand-placing. the ✦ is your track.

drones you've visited

How the verdict is built. Each of the eight dials adds points to a running tally for ~40 artists; the highest score wins, and the two nearest runners-up show up as also bordering on. The suggested track title is generated live in that artist's real naming style — tap ↻ another for a fresh one. Why the calls are arguable? Ambient artists borrow from each other endlessly, so a track can sit right between helios and tycho, or cluster and tangerine dream — disagreeing with the oracle is the entire point. Not affiliated with any artist or label; a toy for friends.
